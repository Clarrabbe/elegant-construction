Meet Keith



With over 30 years of experience, Keith Schuler is the proud owner of Elegant Construction, a premier choice for kitchen, bathroom, and interior home remodeling, additions, and ADUs in the Monterey Bay area. Since 1992, Keith and his team have built a reputation for exceptional craftsmanship, customer service, and a commitment to turning clients’ visions into reality. Customers trust Keith and Elegant Construction not only for their expertise in framing, plumbing, tile work, and fine carpentry but also for their attention to detail and personalized approach. Keith and his team believe in open communication, clear timelines, and transparency at every step. Their focus on quality and care means every project is completed to the highest standard, creating spaces clients love for years to come. Known for their friendly, approachable attitude, Keith and his staff make remodeling an enjoyable, stress-free experience. With a wholesome approach that centers on client satisfaction, they ensure every detail aligns with the client’s vision. For a free consultation and estimate, contact us today and let Keith and his team help bring your dream home to life.
